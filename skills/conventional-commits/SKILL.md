## What I do
- Enforce the Conventional Commits spec (feat:, fix:, chore:, docs:, refactor:, test:, perf:)
- Keep commits small and focused on a single logical change
- Suggest a concise, imperative subject line (<= 72 chars) and a body when needed

## When to use me
Use this whenever staging and committing changes, especially before pushing or
opening a pull request.
